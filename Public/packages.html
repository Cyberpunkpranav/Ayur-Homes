Accomodation

The refereshing air, construction combining stones and wood keep us connected to Nature and rooms designed for air flow are a highlight of AyurHomes. All elements have been carefully curated to provide ethnicity to your stay. With carved ceilings, doors and windows and wooden balcony railings, it is certain to transfer you to the ancient days village life. The extension of this ethnic recreation can be seen in the cupboards and chairs made of bamboo cane, wooden ceilings and doors, balconies and corridors in ancient style. Adding to these specialized facilities are our heritage rooms which accommodate treatment rooms, steam bath room and flower bath room

Spacious rooms and surroundings have a peaceful ambience of their own, which has been taken care at AyurHomes. Ancient craftsmanship has been carefully recreated for you to experience the age old treatment of Ayurveda. Along with aesthetic beauty, it is made sure that your stay is luxurious. All modern amenities have been included while preserving the traditional charm. Here, the best modern amenities are set in ancient royal structures, making it a unique destination of its own. Come and experience this royal retreat where you enjoy your own space and have world class facilities, making your treatment the most relaxed and reviving experience.
